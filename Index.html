Hello, welcome to my for honor guide. What seperates my guide from the rest is my brutal honesty when it comes to something I love. Instead of overloading you with a bunch of information that won't only not help you, it would probably just confuse you. For example I won't tell you to grind a a particular overused game mode even if you get good rewards. And I won't tell you to not play a certain person because their too "easy" or "cheap". I will show you what to really play and who to really play. And I will tell you what game modes give big boy amounts of exp and which are a waste of valuble time, and finally I will tell you what people to play and which to avoid. And remember just because it's cheap, doesn't mean it doesn't work.
